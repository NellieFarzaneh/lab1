Hello, my name is Nellie

I'm a hands on UX-designer who loves exploring how people think, feel and interact with digital experiences. I enjoy turnning complex problems infor clear and intuitive solutions, as well as experimenting with ideas that make products feel enjoyable and engaging, and also looks good. I like to learn new things with pototyping, reseraching and everything in between.

Biography

When I was younger I lived in Skåne. There I studied animal health care. I chose this major as I have always had an interest and compassion for animals. I remember that I as a child always wanted to be around animals and was sad that my brother was allergic to fur animals, and was very jealous of friends and family members who had pets. My plan after Gymnasiet was to continue with animal related studies, such as veterinary medicine or animal welfare inspector. However, this meant I had to move to a city that I did not want to move to so after a lot of consideration I decided to start my IT journey as a interaction designer. I am currently studying my third, and last year, as an interaction designer at Linneus University in Kalmar. I found my interets for this program becuase of my boyfriend. When we met he was studying the same thing, and i was originally planning on studying economics. After seeing what my boyfriend did I was intrigued and started looking into the program myself. I found it very interesting and decided to apply.

Interets

Goals

I would like to work at a company with a sustainability focus. I would like my future working place to have a good work environment where I can learn and grow as a designer.
